Security Center
The Security Center is the fortress of STEINZ a comprehensive suite of AI powered protection tools that
scan, analyze, and alert on every potential threat before it can harm your funds. Security is not a feature
on this platform. It is the foundation every interaction is built on.
5.1 Token Safety Scanner
Enter any token contract address and receive an instant comprehensive safety analysis. The platform
returns a Trust Score from 0 to 100 covering contract verification, liquidity lock details, holder
distribution, buy and sell tax breakdown, developer wallet history, honeypot simulation, and a final
verdict of SAFE, CAUTION, or DANGER with a full written explanation.
5.2 Contract Analyzer
Paste any contract address and VTX AI analyzes the ABI and bytecode, then explains what the contract
does in plain English. It flags owner and admin functions, pause mechanisms, mint authority, blacklist
functions, and hidden fees everything you need to know before you sign anything.
5.3 Rug Detector
The most advanced rug pull detection in Web3. Enter any token or deployer address and receive a
complete risk profile: full timeline of past deployments, serial rugger identification with pattern matching,
liquidity removal behavior analysis, historical success and failure rates, and honeypot simulation results.
5.4 Phishing Detector
Enter any URL or wallet address and instantly verify its safety. The platform checks domain age,
registration information, cross references against known scam databases, shows community reports,
and gives a clear verdict of SAFE, SUSPICIOUS, or PHISHING with a direct recommendation.
5.5 Wallet Approval Manager
Connect your wallet and see every single token approval ever granted the approved contract, the limit,
the date, and a risk rating. Any suspicious approval can be revoked with one click. A Revoke All
Dangerous button handles bulk cleanup instantly. High-risk approvals are highlighted in red so they
cannot be missed.
5.6 MEV Protection Dashboard
Toggle MEV protection on and off and monitor your protection status in real time. When active, the
dashboard shows the count of MEV attacks blocked, the dollar amount saved from sandwich attack
prevention, private RPC routing status, gas optimization statistics, and live protection alerts.
